# NetHydra Code of Conduct

## Purpose

NetHydra is an open community and project focused on cybersecurity auditing, monitoring, digital forensics, threat intelligence, and Security Operations Center (SOC) activities.

We are committed to creating a professional, inclusive, and respectful environment for contributors, users, researchers, students, and organizations.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment include:

- Treating others with respect and professionalism.
- Sharing knowledge constructively and responsibly.
- Providing feedback in a helpful and objective manner.
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Supporting collaboration, learning, and continuous improvement.
- Following ethical and legal cybersecurity practices.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Hate speech or abusive language.
- Publishing private, confidential, or sensitive information without authorization.
- Deliberately spreading misinformation.
- Spam, trolling, or disruptive conduct.
- Encouraging illegal, unethical, or harmful activities.

## Responsible Use

NetHydra is intended to support:

- Security auditing and assessment.
- Security monitoring and observability.
- Digital forensics and incident response.
- Threat intelligence research.
- Security operations and defensive cybersecurity practices.

Community platforms, documentation, and project resources must not be used to:

- Coordinate unauthorized access to systems.
- Promote malicious cyber activity.
- Share stolen data or credentials.
- Distribute malware or harmful content.

## Community Responsibilities

Maintainers are responsible for fostering a healthy community and may take appropriate action when violations occur.

Actions may include:

- Private warning.
- Content removal.
- Temporary participation restrictions.
- Permanent removal from community spaces.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all NetHydra community spaces, including:

- Source code repositories.
- Documentation.
- Issue trackers.
- Pull requests.
- Discussion forums.
- Chat and collaboration platforms.
- Official social media channels.

## Reporting

Violations may be reported to the NetHydra maintainers through official project communication channels.

Reports will be reviewed fairly and handled confidentially whenever possible.

## Enforcement

Maintainers reserve the right to take action necessary to protect the community, project integrity, and participants.

## Acknowledgements

This Code of Conduct is inspired by established open-source community standards and adapted for the NetHydra project.
